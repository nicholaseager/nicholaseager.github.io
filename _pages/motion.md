After hiking around the world in unbelievable places for years, I began to notice one pattern: When I went home, it was impossible to describe the beauty of these places to the people that I love. I began with a thought to fix this. But as I started filming more and more hikes, I noticed how it changed me. Filming a hike helps me slow down and notice the details, much like I used to when I was just starting out. More often than not, when I am hiking ultralight, I tend to speed towards the goal and miss the most beautiful moments. Taking the time to notice these moments, and being able to bring friends and family along with me on my journeys drives me to film.
